ABOUT US

Even Micron Matters!

We started our company with just one manual tool grinding machine back in 2003. In those days were the regrind industry used to be all touch and feel.

We had manual grinders who did it by hand. Over time the need for tighter tolerances has increased as manufacturers have required more precise regrinding processes.

OUR SERVICES

  • Tool CNC Regrinding Services

    Our regrinding service returns tools to ‘as new’ condition. Quality is guaranteed from the CNC grinding machines which are operated by highly experienced personnel, using advanced technology.

  • Tool Manufacture

    Sometimes you need a tool to complete a job that is not commercially available. No worries! Shalom Tool Grinding can make your tool from your drawing. No matter if you want to cut out an operation or two - or need to get into a difficult location, we can make you a tool to accomplish what you want to do. Email or Send Your drawing and we'll make it happen.

  • Tool Coating Services

    Shalom is the only company that provides CNC Tool Regrinding And coating service All Under One Roof. Free pickup and delivery of tools in all over Mumbai area.

    There are some serious benefits of Tool Coating, Here We Will Share Some, Longer Tool Life – 300% To 1000% Increases Are Typical Compared To Uncoated. Increased Productivity – Tools Can Be Run At Higher Feeds And Speeds, Tool won't Be vibrate.

    Uniform Thickness
    Will Not Alter Critical Dimensional Tolerances Of Components Or Parts.
    Corrosion Resistant
    Thermally And Chemically Stable. Not Affected By Most Acids And Alkalis.
    Smoother Work Piece Surfaces
    One Half The Co-Efficient Of Friction Of Uncoated Surfaces Results In Better Surface Finish In Machining And Better Mould Release In Casting Operations.
    Lower Maintenance Costs
    The Significant Increase In Tool Life Means Fewer Tool Changes And Less Down Time.More Regrinds Possible Due To The Wear Land Being Significantly Reduced On Coated Tools.
